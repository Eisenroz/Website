My Favourite Type Face

This is my personal favourite typeface, the letter Mm. This typeface just has a great shape, design, and adaptability.

This typeface is designed to show off the font and the font name, to make people wonder "what is that font?" it showcases and highlights the type of font in which I don't think the other typefaces does.
